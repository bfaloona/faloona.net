A Tibetan Lama (left, with drum) blessing a local hotel.  The evening we arrived in Dunshe from Kathmandu, I stumbled upon a beautiful ceremony.  After hours of listening to gentle chants, surrounded by sincere, loving people, I faded toward sleep.  As I departed for the evening, the ceremony seemed to move without time. 

The lama chanted until the next day.

Tibetan Hotel, Dunshe, Nepal
