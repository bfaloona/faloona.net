bio

born in dallas texas on march 27, 1968 to freddi (linda) perin fredrickson, a nursing student and future film maker, and gerald raymond faloona, an irish biochemistry student and political activist. thanks to bi-costal parents, i moved frequently between dallas tx, san francisco and orange county ca. passionate about learning and good work but never a quick study, i continue to get by on my thin wit and careful listening.

acting, and the art of theatre, grabbed my attention pre-highschool because it embraced the human desire to play. although i have no immediate plans to return to the field, theatre provided the most challenging human experience i have witnessed.
